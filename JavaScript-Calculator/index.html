If you're using your PC keyboard on this app, here are some tips to help you out:

  • 👉🏽 To turn a positive number into a negative one use the "Shift + _" shortcut after you fininshed typing the number. Note that if you are using your mouse instead, to turn a positive number into its negative you will need to click this button after you finished entering the number.
  • 👉🏽 Use the Backspace key to delete the last typed digit or operator from the calculator's display.
  • 👉🏽 Use the Delete key to clear the calculator's display entirely.

All the other buttons on the calculator app are identical to the ones on your computer keyboard.

Have fun testing out this app and if you have any suggestions feel free to send them my way. 😉
